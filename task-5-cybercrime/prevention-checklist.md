# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students)
# General Safety Tips
* Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible

* Never share personal information (OTP, passwords, ID details) with anyone online
* Verify links before clicking = avoid unknown or suspicious URLs
* Keep your devices updated with the latest software and security patches
* Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into sensitive accounts on open networks
* Adjust privacy settings on social media platforms like Instagram and Facebook to control who can see your information
# Financial / UPI Safety Tips
* Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone = banks never ask for it
* Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept “collect requests” without verifying the sender
* Always double-check the receiver’s name before sending money via apps like Google Pay or PhonePe
# Reporting (Important)
* If something goes wrong, report it quickly on the National Cyber Crime Portal:
* https://cybercrime.gov.in
* You can also call 1930 (cybercrime helpline, works 24x7)
